Population Growth of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints





As of 2024, The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints it's clear to see that it IS a worldwide Church!

  • Total Church Membership: 17,509,781
  1. North America: 9,733,719
  2. South America: 4,392,463

  • Congregations: 31,676

  • Full-time Teaching Missionaries: 74,127

  • Temples: 367

God is hastening His work. Hurrah for Gathered Israel!!!
